A Special Dua

May Allah keep this bond sincere and rooted in goodness.
May He protect it from harm, distance, and hardship.
May He fill it with patience, understanding, and mercy.
May He make it a source of peace in difficult days.
May He strengthen it through time and change.
May He keep it pure in intention and gentle in presence.
May He surround it with barakah and calm.
May He guide it whenever hearts feel uncertain.
May He preserve what is good between us always.
And may He place affection and mercy between hearts, as He says:

“And He placed between you affection and mercy.”
— Qur’an 30:21
